Overview

Validation

You want to be sure you've written valid markup. Use the online validator tool to check your work. If there are errors, read what the validator says about them and check the lines in your markup where the errors occur. Watch the validation video for reminders about how the validator works and how to make corrections. Most errors in your markup show up as several errors in the validator because of a sort of cascading effect. Once you fix an error, several of the validator errors will probably be resolved.

Remember that you will get errors for the border attribute. That's okay for now, but later when we learn more complex CSS, the error will not be okay. Any other errors must be fixed.
